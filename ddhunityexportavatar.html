Exporting the avatar

For more information on exporting avatars, click here.

1. Using the menu bar, navigate to DDH>Export Avatar

2. Click the drop-down list Choose Avatar To Export and choose either Unity Video Player or AV Pro Video Player

3. Click the circle next to None (DDH Video Animation Controller) and select the prefab asset created (eg. LauraV5_01)

4. Click Find Dependencies and select the files to be exported with the avatar

5. Click Export to Folder and choose a folder
