Dynamic Digital Humans Documentation

About

The Dynamic Digital Humans (DDH) tool enables quick and efficient integration of the DDH workflow into Unity. It is designed to easily import and export avatars and play its animations. There are two demos available along with the tool to test the avatars in both VR and Desktop environments.

For more information about the Unity Engine intergration, visit the Unity Setup page.

How It Works

With just a few clicks, your .fbx character will be set up to use the Dynamic Digital Humans avatar pipeline, where you can assign the animation clips and video atlas videos you want to use.

Refresh the animations through the DDH tool, hit play, and watch your character come to life!
